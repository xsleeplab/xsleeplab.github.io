Principal Investigator

Xia Tao 夏涛

Principal Investigator

State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Science and Mental Health
Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IPCAS)

Dr. Tao Xia received his Ph.D. from the University of Hong Kong (HKU), where he studied sleep-based memory modulation and targeted memory reactivation (TMR). He is now a Principal Investigator at the Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research lies at the intersection of sleep neuroscience, emotional memory, and brain-computer interfaces, with the goal of developing non-invasive tools to reshape memory and improve mental health.
