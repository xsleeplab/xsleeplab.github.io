Working with us

Why Join X-Sleep Lab?

X-Sleep Lab fosters a collaborative, supportive environment at the forefront of sleep and cognitive neuroscience. Our lab provides state-of-the-art EEG infrastructure, close mentorship, and clear pathways to publishing in high-impact journals. We are based at the Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing.

PhD Students

Ph.D. Positions

We welcome applications from motivated students with a background in psychology, neuroscience, biomedical engineering, or computer science. Successful candidates will join the CAS PhD program and work on sleep-based memory modulation, closed-loop BCI, or lucid dream neuroscience.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in a related field
  • Strong interest in sleep, memory, or brain-computer interfaces
  • Experience with EEG/neuroimaging or programming (Python/MATLAB) is a plus
  • Good written and verbal communication skills

To apply, please send your CV, a brief statement of research interest, and contact information for two references to the PI.

Research Assistants

Research Assistant Positions

We periodically recruit full-time and part-time research assistants to support our ongoing projects. Responsibilities include EEG data collection, participant recruitment, behavioral testing, and data preprocessing.

Undergraduate Researchers

Undergraduate Opportunities

Undergraduates with genuine interest in sleep and memory research are welcome to reach out. We offer research internship opportunities and thesis project supervision.
